Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is one of the most powerful beings in the world. It's incredible just how powerful it is. It's ancient mysteries are only understood by Italians and Glaswegian chip shop owners called Federico.

This creature can be found deep within the Stromboli Caves of Mount Sicily, on the west coast of Nigeria. Approach with caution. Do not startle. I repeat, do not startle. They are fast, vicious, and nine meters tall.

Ingredients

  • A gun
  • Four pints of milk
  • A dog whistle
  • An old fashioned Tesco carrier bag
  • Tuesday

Steps

  1. Wait until tuesday. Lasagna only feeds on a Tuesday
  2. You must pour four pints of milk onto the sand. The cave will open
  3. You will not see any lasagnas. Blow the dog whistle, they will appear
  4. Fire the gun. This will startle the lasagnas
  5. Grab one in the plastic bag and run as fast as you can
